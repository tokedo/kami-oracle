Notes
-----
- Chain returns UPPERCASE strings drawn from
  {EERIE, NORMAL, SCRAP, INSECT}. We store verbatim — no lowercasing,
  no title-casing, no remapping. Founder will adapt downstream queries
  to whatever case the chain uses.
- 30 distinct body trait indices, 27 distinct hand trait indices vs.
  4 affinities — body/hand→affinity is many-to-one. The new columns
  are denormalized for ergonomics; the integer body/hand columns
  remain alongside.
- Sample diversity: bodies 0, 11, 14 all give different affinities
  (SCRAP, EERIE, NORMAL respectively), confirming body→affinity is a
  real lookup, not a single-affinity world.
- The existing `kami_static.affinities` JSON column already carries
  the same chain data Session 9 fetched. Session 12 splits it into
  two scalar columns so agents can JOIN/GROUP BY without parsing JSON.
